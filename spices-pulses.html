    Spices, Pulses & Gram (Empowering Farmers and Village Women)

    Elevator: Beyond fish and poultry, Manvi fosters self-reliance by enabling farmers and village women to cultivate and process spices, pulses, and gram. With modern value-addition, packaging, and AI-driven market access, this initiative boosts household incomes and strengthens rural economies.

    Traditional Indian inputs we keep

    • Age-old cultivation of turmeric, chili, mustard, coriander, and pulses in village farms.
    • Household-level processing methods: hand pounding, sun drying, and traditional stone grinding.
    • Community-based labor where women contribute significantly to post-harvest handling.

    Modern + AI features

    • AI-powered crop advisory (soil suitability, sowing times, pest prediction).
    • Smart irrigation and IoT sensors for pulses/gram crop optimization.
    • Women-led micro-units for hygienic cleaning, grading, and packaging.
    • AI-assisted market linkage platform ensuring best prices and demand forecasting.
    • Branding with QR traceability showcasing “Handcrafted by Village Women.”
    • E-commerce integration for direct-to-consumer premium spice & pulse packs.

    Benefits

    • Additional income streams for rural households.
    • Women empowerment through skill training, micro-enterprise ownership, and leadership roles.
    • Premium, farm-fresh spices and pulses directly reaching consumers.
    • Local-to-global brand narrative: “Authentic, Ethical, Empowered.”

    Implementation checklist

    • Train farmers and women’s self-help groups (SHGs) in modern spice & pulse cultivation + hygiene standards.
    • Set up small-scale solar dryers, grinders, and packaging units in villages.
    • Deploy EMS-powered digital training modules for women entrepreneurs.
    • Launch a consumer brand line (spices, pulses, gram) highlighting rural women empowerment.
    • Integrate with Manvi’s farm-to-table restaurants and online platforms for assured markets.
