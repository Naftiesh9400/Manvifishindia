Fish Egg Solutions (Seed & Hatchery Services)

Elevator: Healthy seed, healthy harvest — Manvi provides high-quality, disease-free fish eggs and hatchery solutions to farmers. With a mix of traditional hatchery wisdom and modern AI-driven monitoring, we ensure stronger survival rates and higher yields.

Traditional Indian inputs we keep

  • Indigenous hatchery methods for Indian Major Carps (Rohu, Catla, Mrigal) and regional fish varieties.
  • Controlled breeding in village ponds using eco-friendly methods.
  • Community-based hatchery networks supplying fingerlings to local farmers.

Modern + AI features

  • AI-powered hatchery management system monitoring temperature, oxygen, pH, and ammonia in real time.
  • Computer vision to track egg development, detect deformities, and predict hatching success.
  • Hormone-induced breeding protocols standardized for higher hatch rates.
  • Blockchain-enabled traceability of fish seed batches for farmer confidence.
  • Mobile app integration — farmers can pre-book seed, track hatchery status, and receive alerts on delivery.
  • AI-predictive demand planning for seed supply, reducing shortages and wastage.

Benefits

  • Higher hatching success → more fingerlings for farmers.
  • Reduced mortality due to better-quality, certified seed.
  • Increased farmer trust with traceable and guaranteed healthy fish eggs.
  • Stronger farm yields → more profitability for aquaculture entrepreneurs.

Implementation checklist

  • Upgrade hatcheries with IoT sensors for water quality.
  • Train hatchery workers with EMS project-based modules on modern breeding.
  • Establish “Manvi Certified Seed” as a trusted quality brand.
  • Build cold-chain logistics for safe seed/fry transport.
  • Create partnerships with state fisheries departments and export buyers.
