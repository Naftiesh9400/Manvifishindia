Chilli, Onion & Garlic (Production, Export & Farmer Enablement)

Elevator: India’s most essential kitchen staples — chilli, onion, and garlic — grown with sustainable practices and scaled for export markets. Manvi connects farmers to global demand while providing training, technology, and fair trade opportunities.

Traditional Indian inputs we keep

  • Multi-generational expertise in chilli cultivation (Andhra, Maharashtra, Rajasthan), onion growing (Nashik, Karnataka), and garlic farming (Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan).
  • Traditional sun-drying, rope-braiding of garlic, and local seed-saving methods.
  • Seasonal cycles aligned with monsoon and winter cropping.

Modern + AI features

  • AI-powered crop advisory (predicting sowing windows, fertilizer balance, pest alerts).
  • Smart irrigation and soil moisture sensors to optimize water use.
  • Grading and sorting automation with image recognition for export quality compliance.
  • Blockchain-based traceability for global buyers.
  • AI-driven market intelligence to match international demand (chilli powder, dehydrated onion flakes, garlic paste).
  • Export-ready packaging and logistics integration with cold chain solutions.

Benefits

  • Farmers receive better global market prices and reduced reliance on local traders.
  • Export branding creates a premium segment for “Manvi Assured Spices & Staples.”
  • Consistency in size, color, and quality → stronger buyer trust internationally.
  • Direct empowerment of farmers through training and contract farming opportunities.

Implementation checklist

  • Identify farmer clusters specializing in chilli, onion, and garlic.
  • Deploy EMS training modules for Good Agricultural Practices (GAP) and export standards.
  • Set up grading, dehydration, and packaging units at local hubs.
  • Create export tie-ups with Middle East, Europe, and Southeast Asia buyers.
  • Brand products with QR-enabled traceability and rural empowerment messaging.
