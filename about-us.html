ABOUT US MANVI FISH AND DUCK FARMING INDIA PVT LTD

Manvi Fish and Duck Farming India Pvt. Ltd. is a part of the portfolio of Sharma Group of Companies, known for successful implementation of sustainable scale projects in fish, poultry, duck, animal husbandry and agriculture related sectors in India. Manvi Fish and Duck Farming India Pvt. Ltd. is recognized as a listed commercial incubator of fish farming entrepreneurs of India. It focuses on five major sectors: fisheries, poultry, duck farming, animal husbandry and agriculture. These businesses are a balanced combination of well-established and emerging enterprises, dedicated to meets the emerging needs of India.

Manvi Fish and Duck Farming India Pvt. Ltd. Company is committed to make the agriculture scenario of India more vast by empowering the farmers with new resources in the field of fish, poultry, duck, animal husbandry and agricultural activities. Rooted in tradition and inspired by innovation, we provide a wide range of services and resources to improve the livelihoods of villagers, promote environment friendly practices and boost economic growth.
